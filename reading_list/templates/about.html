About the Reading List Manager Application

Welcome to the Reading List Manager Application, created as part of the COMP 3005 Final Project. This application is designed to help users manage, customize, and organize their reading and study lists effectively. It supports collaboration, progress tracking, and sharing, making it an ideal tool for students, researchers, and book enthusiasts.

Features:

  • Customize multiple reading lists.
  • Add books with details like ISBN, title, author, genre, and publication year.
  • Search books by title, author, genre, or user-generated tags.
  • Track reading progress with statuses like "in progress" or "completed".
  • Add personal notes and set reading goals to enhance productivity.
  • Collaborate with others by sharing lists or inviting contributors.
  • Control privacy settings to keep lists public or private.
